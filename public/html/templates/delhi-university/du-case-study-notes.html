How to Use These Case Study Notes

This file is designed for Delhi University subjects where application-based questions are important. It helps students understand how to read a case, identify the concept, apply the rule, and write a proper conclusion.

Best Use

Read the case carefully, underline important facts, identify the concept, apply the relevant rule, and then write a clear conclusion.

Case Answer Formula

Case Facts → Relevant Concept → Rule / Principle → Application → Conclusion.

1. Case Study Solving Method

Step What to Do Exam Purpose
Step 1 Read the case carefully and identify important facts. To understand what the question is asking.
Step 2 Identify the relevant concept, law, rule, principle, or formula. To connect the case with the syllabus.
Step 3 Explain the rule or principle briefly. To show conceptual clarity.
Step 4 Apply the rule to the facts of the case. To answer the actual case situation.
Step 5 Write the final conclusion clearly. To complete the answer properly.

8. Common Mistakes in Case Study Answers

  • Writing only theory without applying it to the case.
  • Not identifying the correct concept or rule.
  • Ignoring important facts given in the case.
  • Writing conclusion without proper explanation.
  • Copying the question language without analysis.
  • Writing irrelevant points to increase answer length.
  • Not using headings in long case answers.

Mistake Avoidance Tip

Before writing the final answer, ask: “Which concept is tested, which rule applies, and what is the conclusion?”

  • I wrote a clear conclusion.

  • I used headings where required.
