AS 2 — Inventory Valuation
Lower of Cost or NRV
Prudence (Conservatism) Principle
SLM Depreciation
(Cost – Scrap) × Rate %
Same amount every year
WDV Depreciation
Opening WDV × Rate %
Decreasing amount each year
FIFO — Closing Stock
Always from latest purchases
Oldest stock sold first
Weighted Average
New Avg = Total Cost ÷ Total Units
Recalculate after EVERY purchase
Normal Loss
Reduce quantity only
No cost impact (evaporation, leakage)
Abnormal Loss
Remove quantity & cost
Charge to P&L (fire, theft)
Profit/Loss on Sale
Sale Price – WDV on sale date
Positive = Profit Credit to P&L
Depreciation Rule: Machine purchased before 1st April = Full year; after 1st October = Half year.
Unit 3 · Final Accounts & NPOs
COGS (Cost of Goods Sold)
Opening Stock + Purchases + Direct Expenses – Closing Stock
Direct Expenses = Wages, Carriage Inward, Freight
Gross Profit
Sales – COGS
Profit from core trading activities
Net Profit
Gross Profit + Other Incomes – Other Expenses
Final profit after all expenses
Balance Sheet
Assets = Liabilities + Capital
Always balances!
Closing Capital
Opening Capital + Net Profit + Interest on Capital – Drawings – Interest on Drawings
Adjust for owner's transactions
NPO — Subscription
Received – Outstanding(beg) – Advance(end) + Outstanding(end) + Advance(beg)
"Jo mila + jo aaya – jo tha – jo aage ka mila + jo pehle ka tha"
NPO — Stationery Consumed
Opening Stock + Purchases – Closing Stock
Purchases = Cash Paid + Creditors(end) – Creditors(beg)
NPO — Capital Fund
Opening Capital Fund + Surplus + Capital Receipts – Capital Expenses
Life Membership, Legacies, Specific Donations
AdjustmentP&L / TradingBalance Sheet

Prepaid ExpenseSubtract from expenseAsset
DepreciationDebit P&LDeduct from Asset
Bad DebtsDebit P&LDeduct from Debtors

Golden Rule: Every adjustment has TWO effects — one in P&L/Trading and one in Balance Sheet!

Branch — Profit/Loss

Debit side bigger = Loss

1/3 of Invoice Price

GIL — Gross Investment in Lease
(Rental × Years) + GRV + UGRV
Total money lessor will receive
UFI — Unearned Finance Income
