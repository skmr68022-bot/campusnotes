Best Answer Format

13. Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Do not write vague or incomplete definitions.
  • Do not mix two similar concepts without explaining the difference.
  • Do not write long paragraphs without headings.
  • Do not ignore examples where examples are required.
  • Do not skip conclusion in long-answer questions.
  • Do not write memorized content without connecting it to the question.
